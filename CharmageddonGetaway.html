Charmageddon Getaway

Overview

You used to be depressed but obtained a shady charm potion. This somehow made you irresistible to everyone. Try to get away, as they are coming for you. Made for the 72 hours Winter Melon Jam 2023 where the theme was "charm" in a group of 4 people.

Personal contributions

After the shared contibutions of the game design, my main task was designing and making the map. Providing interesting locations while making sure the player can never reach any place they aren't supposed to reach. Once that was done, I focues my attention to make sure the webbuild worked as intended, taking into consideration how well the game ran and optimizing where necessary.

Furthermore I worked on the core game loop, the UI and various VFX. In the later stages I helped with bugfixing, guiding playertesting and doing the voices for several of the soundeffects.
