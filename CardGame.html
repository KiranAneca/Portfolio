Card game

Overview

A 1v1 online card game where your resource to play cards is also your win condition. This is currently a work in progress, a lot of the visuals are subject to change. It is currently in a prototyping phase, where different cards and ideas are being tested.

Gameplay

A card game, where your resources to play cards is also your win condition. Play the cards in a 2x3 and make clever use of placement to reap the best rewards possible.

Positioning is everything in this game. A plethora of keywords change the way you interact with cards. Most cards interact in some way with the cards around them. Examples are protecting cards in the backline, or empowering cards to the sides.

Behind the scenes

CCG Kit is used as a base to start out from for the quick prototyping. This framework is expanded upon to accommodate for the positional logic and the unique abilities. CCG Kit uses mirror as a networking solution. For the matchmaking, FizzySteamworks This way, the game can be tested using steam's P2P networking. is used.
