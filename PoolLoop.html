PoolLoop

Overview

A game made as part of the 2 Games a Month program. A pool game with a twist. Your ball changes color on contact, but you can no longer hit balls of your color. Figure out multiple puzzles using this key mechanic to clear the puzzle in the least amount of shots possible.

Core mechanics

The core mechanic is the color changing and the color interaction. However there are a lot of supporting mechanics like: erasers, trails, colored holes etc.

Game design

The initial design of the mechanics stayed quite true to the first drafts, however I also worked on the potential expansion of the full game including different modes and new mechanics.
